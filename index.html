    Welcome.
    Press ' START ' to begin a new game.
    NAME
      • Required two players.
      • Player 1 will start the game by entering a word.
      • Player 2 will follow by entering a word beginning with the last letter of player 1's word.
      • You both have 60 seconds until the game end.
      • Your turn will end if you entered a word that is already entered or your word did not start with a set letter.
      • Player will score one point for each word entered.
      • Player will score double points for words with 10 or more letters.
